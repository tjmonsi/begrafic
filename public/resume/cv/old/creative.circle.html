I am interested in applying for the Bilingual Art Director position.  I received my Bachelor of Arts degree from CSULB in Journalism/Public Relations, with a Minor in Spanish, and completed coursework at Art Center College of Design.  I have been working in web and graphic design for the past twelve years, while keeping abreast of the latest software and design techniques.  In addition, as a native Spanish speaker, I was responsible for translating documents and facilitating bilingual discussions for Caltech geologists conducting research in Mexico.  At Shopsports.com, I translated all store web sites into Spanish. 

As my resume shows, I am well versed in design programs including Photoshop, Illustrator, Quark, Acrobat, and Dreamweaver.  I believe my web design skills, my project management experience, and my design sense as it applies to typography, photography, and creative imagery will be a valuable asset to your group.

January through July of last year, I traveled through Europe–Sweden, France, Italy, Holland, Spain, Greece, Germany, and Switzerland. During that time, I represented Entergate Communications at the COMDEX computer show in Goteburg, Sweden, developed designs for clients in Italy, Spain, and Holland, and took hundreds of digital photographs of classic architecture, art, and people.  I was privileged to visit many impressive museums like the Louvre in Paris and the Uffizi in Florence. All of these experiences have filled me with fresh creative insights, ideas, and perspectives.

Now that I have returned, I am full of energy and inspiration.  I am seeking full-time work and a chance to settle down.  I look forward to speaking with you after you have had an opportunity to review my qualifications.
