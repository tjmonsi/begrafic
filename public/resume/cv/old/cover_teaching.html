Dear Wayne Baldasso,

I am interested in applying for the graphic design instructor position with Westwood College. Since attending Art Center College of Design in Pasadena and beginning my graphic/web design career many years ago, I have had the opportunity to work for many design firms in a variety of capacities. While I have not taught in an academic setting, I have had vast experience teaching in an industry setting. Not only have I conducted classes in design and software use, but I have worked one-on-one with students in hands-on learning situations, some of which required my bilingual skills. These experiences have increased my desire to become involved in a formal teaching environment where I can share with today's students the skills and knowledge they will need to be successful in graphic design--a field in which I have already worked for nearly a decade.

I have served as project manager for four separate organizations, as well as design instructor for a number of web design firms. As the first designer and webmaster at the California Institute of Technology, I collaborated extensively with staff and faculty in creating and developing promotional material, both online and in print. I also served as designer and editor of the GPS Alumni newsletter. In addition, I have had extensive experience in media planning as well as in writing, editing, and designing various forms of advertisement, news articles, and informative publications. Finally, as a native Spanish speaker, I was responsible for translating documents for GPS geologists conducting research in Mexico and translating an entire web site into Spanish for Shopsports.com.

I am extremely proficient in Photoshop, Image Ready, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and Quark XPress. I am also capable of working on both Windows and Macintosh platforms. I believe my web design skills, my project management experience, and my design sense as it applies to typography, photography, and creative imagery will be a valuable asset to your students.

January through July of this year, I traveled all over Europe–Sweden, France, Italy, Holland, Spain, Greece, Germany, and Switzerland. During that time, I represented Entergate Communications at the COMDEX computer show in Goteburg, Sweden, developed designs for clients in Italy, Spain, and Holland, and took hundreds of photographs of classic architecture, art, and people. I was privileged to visit many impressive museums like the Louvre in Paris and the Uffizi in Florence. All of these experiences have filled me with fresh creative insights, ideas, and perspectives.

Now that I have returned, I am full of energy and inspiration; I am ready for full-time work and a chance to settle down. Thank you for taking the time to review my qualifications. I look forward to speaking with you soon.

Sincerely,
Beatrice Aispuro
