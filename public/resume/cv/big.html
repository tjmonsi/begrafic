Dear John Montgomery

I was referred to you by Stan Diel, who suggested that I speak with you about potential collaborations. I browsed through your web site and was very impressed by the style and quality of your work. I especially enjoyed the slick concept behind your "big" brand. I received my Bachelor of Arts degree from California State University, Long Beach in Journalism/Public Relations and Spanish in 1991 and completed coursework at Art Center College of Design in 1997.

Throughout my fifteen-year career, I have been involved in the planning and management of hundreds of outstanding design and outreach projects.  I have extensive experience designing print and web collateral and re-branding corporate identities. I have developed successful marketing and outreach campaigns, and produced numerous web sites. In addition to superior research and analytical skills, I am capable of managing multiple projects and delivering quality work while meeting deadlines successfully.

As the first webmaster and designer at Caltech, I collaborated extensively with staff and faculty in creating and developing promotional material, both online and in print. I served as designer and editor of the original Caltech Alumni Newsletter, supervised staff and also taught classes in design. Also, as a native Spanish speaker, I translated documents and facilitated bilingual discussions for geologists conducting research in Mexico. I also translated into Spanish several storefront sites for MLB.com, Copeland Sports, and Victory Sweepers, Inc.

I am extremely resourceful, well-organized, proactive, and energetic. I am proficient in Dreamweaver (CSS/HTML), Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Flash. I have a thorough understanding of cross-browser/platform issues, and intuitive UI design. I look forward to speaking with you soon to discuss possible design opportunities.

Sincerely,
